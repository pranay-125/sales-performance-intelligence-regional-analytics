<h1 align="center">Sales Performance Intelligence & Regional Analytics</h1>

<p align="center">
<b>Business Intelligence Case Study | Power BI | Sales Analytics | Profitability Optimization</b>
</p>

<hr>

<h2>📌 Project Context</h2>

<p>
This project presents an end-to-end <b>Sales Performance Intelligence solution</b> developed using <b>Power BI</b> to analyze business performance across regions, products, and customers.
</p>

<p>
The solution transforms raw transactional data into <b>actionable insights</b>, enabling stakeholders to monitor revenue, evaluate profitability, and identify growth opportunities through interactive dashboards.
</p>

<hr>

<h2>🚀 Business Problem</h2>

<p>
Organizations often struggle with <b>fragmented visibility across sales, profitability, and regional performance</b>, making it difficult to identify key revenue drivers and optimize decision-making.
</p>

<p>
There is a need for a centralized analytics solution that provides:
<ul>
<li>Clear visibility into revenue and profit trends</li>
<li>Identification of high-performing products and regions</li>
<li>Insights into customer contribution and behavior</li>
<li>Data-driven support for strategic planning</li>
</ul>
</p>

<hr>

<h2>🎯 Objective</h2>

<p>
To design an interactive business intelligence dashboard that enables <b>data-driven decision-making</b> by analyzing sales performance, profitability, customer contribution, and regional trends.
</p>

<hr>

<h2>📊 Dashboard Preview</h2>

<h3>📊 Executive Overview & Trends</h3>

<p>
<b>Executive Performance Dashboard:</b> 
Tracks key business KPIs including total revenue, profit, profit margin, and order volume to provide a high-level view of business performance.
</p>

<p>
Analyzes <b>monthly revenue and profit trends</b> to identify seasonality patterns and growth momentum, while also evaluating customer spending behavior and pricing efficiency.
</p>

<p>
<b>Business Insight:</b> Enables leadership to monitor financial health, detect trends, and optimize pricing and customer segmentation strategies.
</p>

<h2>📊 Key Metrics</h2>

<ul>
<li><b>Total Revenue:</b> Measures overall business performance</li>
<li><b>Total Profit:</b> Evaluates financial success</li>
<li><b>Profit Margin:</b> Assesses profitability efficiency</li>
<li><b>Units Sold:</b> Tracks product demand</li>
<li><b>Average Selling Price:</b> Evaluates pricing strategy</li>
<li><b>Sales Growth:</b> Measures business expansion over time</li>
</ul>

<hr>

<h2>⚙️ Approach</h2>

<ol>
<li><b>Data Cleaning & Transformation:</b> Processed raw sales data using Power Query</li>
<li><b>Data Modeling:</b> Built a star schema for efficient querying</li>
<li><b>KPI Development:</b> Defined business metrics using DAX</li>
<li><b>Exploratory Analysis:</b> Identified trends and patterns</li>
<li><b>Dashboard Development:</b> Designed interactive visualizations in Power BI</li>
</ol>

<hr>

<h2>🧩 Data Model</h2>

<p>
The solution uses a <b>star schema</b> with a central fact table and supporting dimension tables for optimized performance and scalability.
</p>

<ul>
<li><b>Fact Table:</b> Sales transactions</li>
<li><b>Dimensions:</b> Product, Region, Date</li>
</ul>

<hr>

<h2>🔍 Key Insights</h2>

<ul>
<li>Revenue is concentrated among a small group of high-performing products</li>
<li>West region contributes the highest share of revenue and profitability</li>
<li>Seasonal demand patterns influence monthly revenue trends</li>
<li>Some high-revenue products have lower profit margins</li>
<li>Untapped regions present opportunities for expansion</li>
</ul>

<ul>

<li>Enabled <b>data-driven decision making</b> for business leaders</li>
<li>Identified opportunities for <b>revenue growth and margin optimization</b></li>
<li>Supported strategic planning across products, regions, and customers</li>
</ul>

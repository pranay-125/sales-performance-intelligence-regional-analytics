<h1 align="center">Sales Performance Intelligence & Regional Analytics</h1>

<p align="center">
Business Intelligence Project | Power BI Dashboard | Regional Sales Analysis | Profitability Insights
</p>

<hr>

<h2>Project Overview</h2>

<p>
This project presents an end-to-end <b>Sales Performance Intelligence Dashboard</b> built using <b>Power BI</b> to analyze regional sales performance, profitability trends, and product demand patterns.
</p>

<p>
The objective of this project is to transform raw sales transaction data into actionable business insights that enable organizations to understand revenue drivers, evaluate product performance, monitor regional sales trends, and optimize decision-making through data visualization.
</p>

<p>
The analysis helps identify high-performing markets, profitable products, and seasonal demand patterns, allowing business leaders to make strategic decisions based on data-driven insights.
</p>

<hr>

<h2>Business Objective</h2>

<ul>
<li>Analyze regional sales performance across different markets</li>
<li>Identify high-performing products and categories</li>
<li>Evaluate profitability and profit margin trends</li>
<li>Understand seasonal sales patterns</li>
<li>Monitor revenue contribution across regions</li>
<li>Support data-driven business decision making</li>
</ul>

<hr>

<h2>Key Business Questions</h2>

<ul>
<li>Which regions generate the highest revenue?</li>
<li>Which products contribute most to sales?</li>
<li>How does profitability vary across products and regions?</li>
<li>Are there seasonal patterns in sales performance?</li>
<li>Which customers contribute the highest revenue?</li>
</ul>

<h2>Dataset Overview</h2>

<p>
The dataset represents transactional sales records of a retail organization operating across multiple regions. Each record captures detailed information about product sales, revenue, profits, and geographic location.
</p>

<h3>Key Data Attributes</h3>

<ul>
<li>Order Date</li>
<li>Region</li>
<li>State</li>
<li>Product Category</li>
<li>Product Name</li>
<li>Units Sold</li>
<li>Revenue</li>
<li>Profit</li>
</ul>

<h2>Key Insights</h2>

<ul>
<li>Revenue is concentrated among a small group of high-performing products</li>
<li>The West region contributes the highest share of revenue</li>
<li>Seasonal demand patterns show peak sales during mid-year months</li>
<li>Some products generate high revenue but lower profit margins</li>
<li>Several regions show potential for market expansion</li>
</ul>

<hr>

<h2>Business Recommendations</h2>

<ul>
<li>Increase focus on high-performing products to maximize revenue</li>
<li>Optimize pricing strategies for low-margin products</li>
<li>Expand marketing efforts in underperforming regions</li>
<li>Align promotional campaigns with seasonal demand peaks</li>
<li>Strengthen customer retention strategies for top revenue contributors</li>
</ul>
